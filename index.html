Hello! Let’s meet. I’m a designer from Kyiv, Ukraine. I've got two educations, the first one in economics, and the second one — in advertisement design and computer graphics. I've been designing for over 12 years so far, and during this time I’ve managed to cover all design directions, from graphical and industrial design to motion and sound design. I like comprehensive and complex tasks and unusual solutions. I have great skills in illustration, lettering, branding and web interfaces. Since 2014, I’ve been deep into crypto and finance products and design of complex trading systems. My experience also includes being the founder and co-owner of the Indigital design studio and partner at the INCRYPTICO marketing agency. I have vast experience in management and process organization. My attention to details helps me to bring out my works against others. I am keen to good quality and originality, and they define the standards for my own work. ‘The devil is in the details,’ I use this metaphor in my work and everything I do. Self-organization and communication abilities are my strong qualities. I’m inquisitive and I never stop studying any matter from one point of view, which helps me to find the best solution to any given task. Actuality and innovation in design pay a great deal in my work, I always follow the trends and new possibilities to interact with people. I stick to minimalism and conciseness in design.

What do I do?

Currently, my work is all about fintech projects. I enjoy creating branding and logotypes, developing complex interface solutions based on user experience, solution scenarios, information architecture, product testing, and enhancement. I have strong experience in art direction and managing teams of up to 5 people. I adore illustrations and fonts. I draw by hand perfectly.
